Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

The "Iddu" Privacy Charter: We believe that healthcare data is the most personal information you own. Our architecture is built to process your data, get your chores done, and then get out of the way. We never sell your data, and we provide tools for instantaneous and permanent deletion of sensitive records.

1. Introduction

Iddu Technologies LLC ("Iddu", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

To provide our AI-driven logistics services, we collect various types of data:

  • Account Information: Email address provided during registration or third-party sign-in (Google/Apple).
  • Healthcare Logistics Data: Information from forwarded emails, scanned bills/receipts (EOBs), and voice notes related to your appointments.
  • Communication Data: We record the AI-agent’s interactions when it calls medical offices on your behalf to reschedule or verify appointments.
  • Device Information: Standard technical logs, including IP address, device type, and app usage statistics.

3. AI Data Processing

Iddu utilizes advanced Artificial Intelligence (including Google Gemini) to parse documents and voice recordings. Your data is passed to these models via secure enterprise APIs for the sole purpose of extraction and logic processing. Your personal health information is NOT used to train the general public models of our AI providers.

4. Data Minimization & Retention

We pride ourselves on a "Security-First" approach to data retention:

  • Sensitive Assets: Voice recordings, transcripts, and scanned EOBs/medical bills are permanently hard-deleted from our S3 storage the moment you delete an appointment.
  • Audit Records: A stripped-down metadata record of the appointment (provider name, date) is retained in an "Archived/Deleted" state for 30 days for system integrity before being permanently destroyed.
  • Health Wallet: You have full control over your Health Wallet. Deleting a record from the app removes all associated financial metadata immediately.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information as necessary to provide the Services:

  • Infrastructure Providers: AWS (storage and hosting) and Google (AI parsing).
  • Medical Providers: When you provide explicit consent for Iddu to call an office, we share only the necessary details (your name, date of birth, and appointment request) to complete the task.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including AES-256 encryption at rest (S3 and DynamoDB) and TLS encryption for all data in transit. While we are a logistics assistant, we treat your medical data with the rigor expected of healthcare-adjacent applications.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access the data we hold about you, request corrections, or demand permanent deletion of your account. You can perform these actions directly within the app settings or by contacting us.
